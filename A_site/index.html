About Us

Embrace your taste buds at the beautifully decorated Kalyan Rooftop Restaurant which offers a wide variety of dishes. After a hectic day in the city, just refresh and energise yourself by sitting at the misted rooftop garden restaurant, where every pore of mist will make you feel fresh and lively..... Entertain yourself through the projector system where you could watch live matches or play music. The rooftop restaurant also showcases a beautifully landscaped panoramic 360 degree view of the pinkcity. Just be on the top and mesmerize yourself by seeing the hustle and bustle of the city from gods eye (birds eye). Let our sprinkling lights ensemble to match the ambiance and embrace you with joy.The Kalyan Indoor Restaurant is a beautifully Rajasthani textured restaurant located inside the hotel. The indoor restaurant also known as “The Wooden Restaurant” showcases a beautiful architecture done purely through wood.
